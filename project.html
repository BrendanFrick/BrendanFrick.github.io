Topic Papers

I remember three things that I learned in college: The Representation of the Vichy Woman in Post-War France, Phonemic and Prosodic representations in the Brain, and Statistical Static Timing Analyses in VLSI. These topics were the subjects of the only >10 page papers I wrote as an undergraduate student. I like writing, I miss learning, and I have free time on the weekends so I think committing to an extensive analysis of a single subject would do a lot of good for me.

I am currently working on a review of PFC cognitive function (although there seems to be a pretty popular one already). Once that is posted I hope to take a look at network theory (focus within the field is TBD), before reuniting with my old [not-so] friend quantum physics and quantum signal processing. These latter two are subject to change.
